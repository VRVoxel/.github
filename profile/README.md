# Voxel Research Lab

![Voxel Research Lab](https://img.shields.io/badge/Lab-Immersive%20Tech-blue) ![Lodz University of Technology](https://img.shields.io/badge/University-Lodz%20Tech-green)

**Voxel Research Lab** at Lodz University of Technology brings together academic staff from multiple departments to develop cutting-edge solutions in **Virtual Reality (VR)**, **Augmented Reality (AR)**, and **Mixed Reality (MR)**. The lab is involved in numerous national and international projects focused on immersive technologies.

---

## About Us & Mission

Our mission is to **promote, popularize, and champion immersive solutions** for both casual and professional users. We aim to **raise awareness of the endless possibilities of Extended Reality** and advocate its use beyond entertainment, extending into fields such as:

- Science  
- Medicine  
- Engineering  
- Psychology  

We strongly value **usability and empathy**, emphasizing the creation of **accessible, practical, and user-centered solutions** powered by cognitive science, artificial intelligence, and a deep care for every individual.

---

## Core Areas of Research

Voxel Research Lab operates across several interconnected domains:

### Immersive Technology
- Mixed Reality (MR), Virtual Reality (VR), Augmented Reality (AR)  
- Haptic feedback and interactive environments  

### Human-Computer Interaction
- Human factors engineering  
- Design of computer technology  
- Interaction between humans and computers  
- Information technology design  

### Affective Computing
- Emotion recognition  
- Stress monitoring  
- Facial expressions, gestures, speech signals  
- EEG and bio-signal analysis  

### Machine Learning
- Deep learning and artificial intelligence  
- Artificial neural networks  
- Classification and clustering algorithms  

### Cognitive Science
- Creating systems that respond to human thought  
- Psychology, neuroscience, language studies  
- Integration of AI, philosophy, and anthropology  

### Universal Design
- Designing for all, including elderly and cognitively/physically impaired  
- Accessibility and inclusion for diverse human experiences
